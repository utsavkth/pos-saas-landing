Privacy Policy

We know your shop's sales information is important and personal. Here's a plain explanation of how we handle it, no legal jargon.

1

What we collect

Your store name, email address, and password, so we can run your account.

Your product list, prices, and sales records, so the POS can actually work.

We don't collect anything beyond what's needed to run your shop's POS.

2

Where it's stored

Your data is stored securely on cloud servers, the same kind of infrastructure used by banks and other online services.

We don't keep your data on any single physical device in your shop, so you don't lose your records if a phone or tablet breaks.

3

Who can see it

Only you and the staff you give access to can see your shop's data.

We never share, sell, or mix your shop's information with any other shop's information. Your data is yours.

4

Deleting your data

You can request to delete your account and all associated data at any time.

Just email us at hello@khatiwadapos.com and we'll remove your information, no fees, no hoops to jump through.

Questions about your data? Contact us
