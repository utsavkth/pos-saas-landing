Everything your shop needs, nothing it doesn't

A closer look at what Khatiwada POS actually does.

Scanning

Scan barcodes with your camera

No barcode scanner to buy, no cables, no setup. Point your phone or tablet camera at any barcode and it's added to the sale instantly.

Loose goods

Built for items sold by weight, too

Rice, lentils, spices, and other loose goods work just as easily as packaged items, enter the weight and the price calculates itself. From weighted items to individual items, we've got you covered.

Bilingual

A cashier screen anyone can use

Switch the whole cashier screen between English and Nepali with one tap, so every staff member feels comfortable, not just the tech-savvy ones.

Admin panel

An admin panel that keeps you in control

Monthly sales reports, best-selling item stats, CSV import and export, product photos, and support for multiple staff logins, and much more, everything an owner needs, in one place.

Payments

Cash and QR payments, side by side

Keep accepting cash exactly as you do today, and offer QR-based digital payment as an option, no separate payment device needed.

Coming soon Custom-branded QR codes, like the big marts use, are in the works. This will require registering with Fonepay Business, and we're working with them to get there.
Made simple

Designed for first-time software users

Large buttons, clear steps, and no confusing menus. If you've never used a POS system before, this is where we started designing from.

Works on the device you already have, phone, tablet, or laptop. Any device with a camera and a browser is enough. Prefer a dedicated barcode scanner? You can pair one in, but it's never required.
