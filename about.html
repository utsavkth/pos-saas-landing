Our story

Built for one shop. Now built for yours.

Khatiwada POS didn't start as a company. It started as a spreadsheet, then a small app, built to help one family run their grocery shop in Kathmandu, track what was selling, know what to restock, and stop losing track of the day's cash.

Like a lot of small shops, ours ran on memory, paper notebooks, and a calculator. Buying proper POS hardware felt expensive and complicated for what is, at its heart, a simple business: sell things, know what you sold.

So we built something that works on the phone already sitting behind the counter. No terminal to buy, no technician to call. After using it in our own shop for a while, we realized other shop owners were asking for the same thing, so we're opening it up.

We're still a small team, and we're building this the same way we built the first version, by listening to real shopkeepers, including our own family.

Made by shopkeepers, for shopkeepers: built for every generation, from tech-savvy youth to shop owners who have never touched software like this before.

Let's revolutionise how we think of shops. Anyone can modernise their shop, for the ease of customers and shopkeepers alike.
